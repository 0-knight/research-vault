# FEDS Paper: Enforcing Fair Lending: Evidence from Mortgage Market Litigation

## 요약
Matthew Maury, Michael Suher, and Jeffery Y. ZhangDoes fair lending litigation impact mortgage lender decisions? Using a novel dataset of all fair lending legal actions from 1991 to 2023, we find that it does. In the wake of legal settlements for discrimination against Black borrowers, lenders significantly reduced denial rates for Black applicants.
